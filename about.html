About

I'm a software engineer that strives to learn as much as possible about software development and the technology and tools that go with it. I'm always pursuing new opportunities to better myself in any way possible, whether that's taking on a new job, projects on the side such as working with my personal web server, or developing Android/hybrid mobile applications.

When I'm not working I like to get outside and explore. I usually like to take day trips up to the North Georgia mountains for hikes during the weekends when the weather is nice. If I'm not hiking or enjoying the great outdoors then you can find me at a local brewery with my friends.
